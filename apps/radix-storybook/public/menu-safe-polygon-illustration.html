The submenu
safe polygon

When a submenu opens on hover, it claims a triangular safe zone between the pointer and the popup. You can cut the corner diagonally — straight across the sibling row below — and the submenu stays put. A pointer-events tunnel keeps those siblings inert mid-traverse. Toggle the polygon off to watch the classic flicker: the sibling steals the open submenu.
